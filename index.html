About Me

Greetings! I am a third-year computer engineering student and fortunately, I have gained valuable insights during this academic journey and worked to build a strong scientific foundation. In my research, I focused on software and system security, with a particular interest in delving into the operating system kernel internals, emphasizing reverse engineering, fuzzing, and security.

As I continue my academic journey, I eagerly look forward to the chance to enhance our collective knowledge in this area, Share our research findings and engage in a mutually beneficial learning exchange with community members. I firmly believe that through dedicated research and collaborative efforts, we can make substantial strides in enhancing the security of our computer and software systems.
